About us
ACES is a Community of Computer Engineering Students established with an aim of all round development of students and build a better foundation for their carrier.ACES is an non-profitable organization founded by Computer Engineering students of Purwanchal Campus for the betterment and upliftment of skills of student. Aces helps in pursuing different types of computer oriented skills. It helps students expand their knowledge. It organizes different types of events such as Hackathon, Code-fest, Live-Coding, Debugging, Online Blog Writing, Cloud Computing and many more.
